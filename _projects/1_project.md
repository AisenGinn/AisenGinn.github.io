This ongoing research studies how shared-scene multi-task training shapes latent-space clustering and task interference in visual representations.

Key directions:

- Analyzing latent phase transitions across subgoals in long-horizon robotic tasks.
- Understanding how scene structure changes representation consistency across tasks.
- Proposing scene alignment and task grouping strategies that improve generalization.
